Harry the Dirty Dog

Harry was a white dog with black spots who liked everything, except ... getting a bath.
哈利是一隻有黑色斑點的白狗，他喜歡所有事情，除了……洗澡。
So one day when he heard the water running in the tub, he took the scrubbing brush . . . and buried it in the backyard.
所以有一天，當他聽到浴缸裡流水的聲音時，他拿起刷子……把它埋在後院。
Then he ran away from home.
然後他離家出走了。
He played where they were fixing the street and got very dirty.
他在修馬路的地方玩，弄得非常髒。
He played at the railroad and got even dirtier.
他在鐵路旁玩，變得更髒了。
He played tag with other dogs and became dirtier still.
他和其他狗玩追逐遊戲，變得更髒。
He slid down a coal chute and got the dirtiest of all.
他從煤槽滑下去，變成最髒的樣子。
In fact, he changed from a white dog with black spots to a black dog with white spots.
事實上，他從白底黑點的狗，變成黑底白點的狗。
Although there were many other things to do, Harry began to wonder if his family thought that he had really run away.
雖然還有很多事可以做，哈利開始想：家人是不是以為他真的離家出走了？
He felt tired and hungry too, so without stopping on the way he ran back home.
他又累又餓，所以一路不停地跑回家。
When Harry got to his house, he crawled through the fence and sat looking at the back door.
哈利回到家時，從圍欄鑽進去，坐著看著後門。
One of the family looked out and said,
家人之一往外看，說：
"There's a strange dog in the backyard... by the way, has anyone seen Harry?"
「後院有一隻陌生的狗……對了，有人看到哈利嗎？」
When Harry heard this, he tried very hard to show them he was Harry.
哈利聽到後，非常努力想讓大家知道他就是哈利。
He started to do all his old, clever tricks.
他開始做他以前會的聰明把戲。
He flip-flopped and he flop-flipped.
他翻來翻去，做各種翻轉動作。
He rolled over and played dead.
他翻身打滾，還假裝死掉。
He danced and he sang.
他跳舞，也唱歌。
But everyone shook their heads and said, "Oh no, it couldn't be Harry."
但大家都搖頭說：「喔不，不可能是哈利。」
Harry gave up and walked slowly toward the gate.
哈利放棄了，慢慢走向大門。
Suddenly he stopped and ran to a corner of the garden and started to dig furiously.
突然他停下來，跑到花園角落拼命挖土。
Soon he jumped away barking short, happy barks.
很快他跳開，發出短短快樂的叫聲。
He had found the scrubbing brush!
他找到那把刷子了！
Carrying it in his mouth, he ran into the house.
他把刷子叼在嘴裡，跑進屋子。
He jumped into the bathtub and sat up begging.
他跳進浴缸坐好，像在討好一樣。
"This little doggie wants a bath!" cried the little girl.
「這隻小狗想洗澡！」小女孩喊著。
Harry's bath was the soapiest one he'd ever had.
哈利洗了他這輩子最滿泡泡的一次澡。
It worked like magic.
效果就像魔法一樣。
"It's Harry! It's Harry!" the children cried.
「是哈利！是哈利！」孩子們大喊。
Harry wagged his tail and was very, very happy.
哈利搖著尾巴，非常非常開心。
It was wonderful to be home.
回到家真是太美好了。
